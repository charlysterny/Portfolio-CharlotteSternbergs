Trepol

Product Design

Trepol is a bookend, a furnishing object with the function of holding books. The bookends on the market today appear more as ornamental objects and most of the time do not meet functional and personal needs. Trepol was born in relation to an action that we carry out, when we read the latest book we tend to put it down or put it away in the bookcase, or in a visible place, where we can find it more easily. The same goes for a book we like. Trepol supports books and puts them on display.
