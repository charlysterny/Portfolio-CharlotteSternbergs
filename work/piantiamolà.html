PIANTIAMOLÀ

Product Design / UX/UI

The starting point of this thesis project is a reflection on actual productivity by delving into what remote working or better known as smart working is today. It allowed you to leave space for your daily habits and accelerated skills. The context of remote work was analysed, which can be represented as a large amount of hours of connection, most of the time in solitude, conditioned by abundant distractions, in a substantially cold and insufficiently human reality, given above all by the work tool electronic, i.e. the computer. A circumstance that leads in the long run to worsening the quality of work.


The research and experiments carried out served to study a solution to the problem, thus being able to adopt them for a concrete project proposal. The solution can be found in nature, as the studies and analyzes carried out have demonstrated a relationship between exposure to the natural environment and the psycho-physical well-being of man. The objective of the project is therefore to bring nature into the "home" environment, where in 2022, and thanks to the cultural and work developments of recent years, we spend much more time.


The project is aimed at introducing a system (starter kit) and an application, which allows man to try his hand for the first time in the process of growing the same plant and 'taking care of' it. This, however, does not only lead to the acquisition of skills useful for mere botany, but to develop that relationship between man and nature, which is sought as a solution to well-being while working from home.
