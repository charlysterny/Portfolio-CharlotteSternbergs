Exterior Design / Interior Design

OThe project proposal examines a considerable plot of land located in the hinterland of the south-eastern area of Sicily, close to the center of an ancient agricultural tradition of Pachino based on the cultivation of tomatoes. In recent years, alongside the peculiar agricultural activity of the area, there has been a tourist activity originating from the proximity of various points of interest such as Granelli, the Isola delle Correnti, Punta Cirica and Marza. The area on which the lot is located is part of one of the most important coastal wetland systems in Southern Europe given the presence of the Cuba and Longarini marshes, which have become part of a system of protected areas, namely the 'Pantani of South Eastern Sicily''.


The design intervention in question has the objective of preserving the historical architecture of the buildings and enhancing their pre-existing function. The aim is to convert the abandoned area into a center of tourist interest, where great importance will be given first of all to the adjacent Pantani, but also to the Palmento itself, bringing it back to light and celebrating its function.

Collaboration with: Noemi Cerrato - Luca Veresani
