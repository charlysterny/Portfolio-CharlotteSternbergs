Hot Ciock

Rebranding / Packaging / Product Design / Graphic Design

The request was to take care of the restyling of Hot Ciock. The image chosen had to be appetizing, given that it would be displayed on the shelves of bars and not so much in the hands of the consumer. The restyling includes the logo and packaging of the Hot Ciock line by Segafredo Zanetti.'Enjoying and savoring a warm and dense chocolate on a cold winter day'', hence the thought on the choice of the new naming ''MOMENTI'' for Hot Ciock was born.The campaign was created to strengthen the product of Segafedo Zanetti's hot ciok brand.


Collaboration with: Filippo Giorgini - Laura Ceccardi - Kaoutar Majdouli - Nicola Matteini
