BO-SIGN

Urban Design

Our intervention was born from in-depth research started from the stretch of road of interest to us, Via del Guasto and Via delle Belle Arti (BO), arriving at different aspects of the City, at 360 degrees. The project aims to create a solution that is the result of both people's needs, and also of the territory. BO-SIGN: Through an in-depth analysis of Bologna's urban and social wayfinding signs, we have proposed an Urban Design solution to characterize and bring new identity to the stretch of road of Via delle Belle Arti, which connects the Academy of Fine Arts with the headquarters in Via del Guasto.


Collaboration with: Nicola Pecorari - Nicola Matteini - Noemi Cerrato
