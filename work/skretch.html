Skretch

Product Design

A new container for packaging waste capable of generating new interactions and not leaving it as a simple waste object. The innovation of the product is given by the ability to contain the general dimensions of that waste, in quantitative terms, in a reduced size, thus generating more storage space. All this through a compression system and formal component capable of emotionally creating an interaction of satisfaction, given by the attraction for the product, the fun and satisfaction with what did not previously exist in the waste containers for packaging. A container where the shape and the system relate in a different way, resulting in a very expressive and characteristic element.


The product system was designed for different uses and with different intermediaries. For example, for an adult, simply to compress and block the waste, they push the container through the use of their hands or feet, and then pull the belts to block it. For a child, to have entertainment that is more satisfying, he can compress the garbage through the use of the body, such as sitting down.
