Stand Dominican Republic

Ephemeral architecture / Design of an exhibition

The design proposal of the stand is to evoke and remember the origin through a sort of emotional journey, which accompanies you from the beginning to the end of the stand. All this through the use of colors and local cultural architectural references. To do this, I thought I would reconnect with the voyages that Christopher Columbus made to discover America. Long lines that intersect and which, when analyzed visually, recall a weave of threads, as if recreating a fabric. And it is from there that the intrinsic Dominican culture is born. A journey to remember the story of his birth.
